Asadp

Description

Meat cooked by timber embers.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • salt

Steps

  1. Burn wood until you get embers
  2. Season the meat with salt
  3. Put the meat on the grill
  4. Put some embers under the grill
  5. Cook the meat for about 1:30hs on one side then finish 30 minutes on the other side
