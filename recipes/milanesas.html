Milanesas

Description

The milanesa is a breaded piece of meat (cow, chicken or pork) that is deep fried and served as a main course.

Ingredients

  • Bread crumbs
  • steak
  • eggs
  • parsley
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. mix the eggs with salt, pepper, minced parsley and garlic
  2. soften the meat by hitting it with a maze
  3. put the meat into the eggs mix
  4. cover the egg drenched steak with bread crumbs. Make sure you cover every part of the steak
  5. deep fry in oil at medium temperature
